About Us

Chakra Defense is a leading cybersecurity consultancy based in India, delivering end-to-end cybersecurity solutions to businesses worldwide. Our services are tailored to meet the evolving needs of startups, SMBs, and enterprises, including web application security, mobile app penetration testing, cloud and network security assessments, red teaming, threat modeling, ISO 27001:2022 gap analysis, and vCISO (Virtual CISO) advisory.

At Chakra Defense, we believe cybersecurity goes beyond defense — it’s about building resilience, enabling intelligent risk management, and driving continuous security improvement across your digital ecosystem.

We are a team of certified cybersecurity professionals with over a decade of experience in penetration testing, red teaming, application security, and security operations. We specialize in proactive threat detection, enterprise risk mitigation, and strategic security advisory. By combining deep manual expertise with advanced automated tools, we deliver comprehensive security assessments and monitoring across your digital landscape. Our mission is to help businesses of all sizes protect critical assets and meet regulatory requirements. Our team is backed by industry-recognized certifications from Offensive Security, SANS, and ISC2.

From securing web and mobile apps to red team simulations and ISO/NIST-aligned roadmaps, Chakra Defense delivers precise, tailored cybersecurity solutions. Whether you're a startup or an enterprise, we ensure you feel secure, compliant, and internet-safe.

Backed by industry-leading tools, methodologies, and a deep understanding of attacker behavior, Chakra Defense is your all-round security companion - always on, always ahead.
